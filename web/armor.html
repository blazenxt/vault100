Form 100-M. The armorer's desk — letters sealed to travel

Ordinary vaults are files; armor is a letter — a vault folded into printing-office characters you may paste into any mail, chat or notice board. Written and read with the CLI's --armor as well, byte for byte. Keyfiles are not offered at this desk — armor must travel alone. ≤ 512 KiB per letter.
